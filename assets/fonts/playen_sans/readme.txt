Playen Sans is a new sans serif font with a condensed style as an alternative choice for your typographic designs. This font is a mix of classic and modern styles that is cleanly designed to remain functional, legible and universal.  Playen Sans consists of 5 weights ranging from Light to Black. Some letters even stand out because they are made unique as a characteristic of the font. OpenType features are also included such as stylistic alternates. Apart from supporting multiple languages, Playen Sans also supports Cyrillic characters.  This font is perfect for work related to text, branding, logotype, headlines, editorial and packaging. 

Playen Sans font family is free for both personal and commercial purposes.
